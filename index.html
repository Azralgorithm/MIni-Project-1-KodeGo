GamerZone is a platform that helps people land a job in eSports. As a thriving industry, eSports is a remarkable source of profit in today's generation. Hence, GamerZone is an inititive to help you reach your eSports dream, be it as a gamer, or a professional who serves as a pillar for the industry.

We connect with different eSports organizations and companies worldwide. Using this connection, we inquire about their man power needs and supply them with the right people to carry out the tasks. With this, we help people find opportunities in this industry and help these organizations as well in building a better eSports community.

We aim to create a haven for gamers and other eSports professionals build a harmonious community in eSports embracing unity for a shared victory.
